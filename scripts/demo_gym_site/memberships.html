Frequently asked

Membership FAQ

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes — Standard and Elite members can freeze for up to 60 days per year at no charge. Basic members can freeze for $10/month, up to 30 days per year.

Is there a free trial?

Every new visitor gets one free three-day pass. Book it at the front desk or online with valid ID — no card required.

How do I cancel?

Cancel in person, by emailing membership@ironfit-gym.example, or through the IronFit app. Monthly plans require 14 days' notice. Annual prepayments are non-refundable but transferable.

Are there student or corporate discounts?

Students get 20% off Basic and Standard with a valid ID. Companies with ten or more enrolled employees qualify for a custom corporate rate — contact us for a quote.

Can I bring guests?

Yes. Standard members get one free guest pass per month; Elite members get unlimited guest passes. Drop-in guests can also visit on a $15 day pass.
